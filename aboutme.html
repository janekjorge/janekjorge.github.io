About me

Ever since I was a young child, i've always dreamed of helping other people and always believed medicine
to be my main choice in job career.  Apart from my belief in helping others, i'm constantly online and researching
topics about health that interest me and even worry me. Medicine is just interesting to me, it's as simple as that.

We should all have a secondary plan and even a third plan when it comes to career and if your plan A doesnt work,
then switch to plan B. For my plan B, digital design is becoming an increasingly more popular choice for me considering
the fact that I have learned many design programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere and in the future hope to excel in After Effects, Animate, and other design programs. If something happens with my medicine career path, this is what will turn to for a living. I can also do this as a side job even if i'm into medicine. Theres not a boundary stopping me from  what I want to achieve and i'm focused on my goals and I will complete them.
