Biography

Since being a child, being a doctor has been a dream for me. It has been something I always wanted to become because I want to help people.  A couple years back, I developed a disease that caused me to go into surgery. After I was out of surgery, I began to ponder upon how the doctors saved my life. This struck something with me, and was what led me to want to become a doctor. After I joined middle school, I began to learn programs like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator in my magnet class. This encouraged me to choose graphic design as a secondary career path. I believed that being a doctor for a primary job and owning a graphic design company as a side job was the path I wanted to take in the future.
